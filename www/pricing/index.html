Common questions

What is a production system?

A production system is a distinct application or service you operate — one license covers it entirely, however many servers, environments, or team members are behind it. A few examples:

· A SaaS product with a web app, mobile app, and API → one context (same product, same users)
· All internal tooling your team uses → one context (internal use counts as a single context regardless of how many tools)
· A second product serving a different customer base → separate context

A $2M company shipping one product pays $299/yr total.

What counts as commercial use?

Any use in a product or service that generates revenue, or within a company's internal tooling. Personal projects, open-source libraries, and evaluation are covered by the Community license.

Can I try Lpdf before buying?

Yes. The Community license is free and covers personal, open-source, and commercial use for organizations under $1M annual revenue. No credit card required.

What happens when my license key expires?

PDF generation keeps working — the app never breaks. When a key expires, the attribution line ("Made with lpdf.io") reappears on generated PDFs until you renew. Community and Professional keys expire after one year; Professional keys renew automatically with your subscription. Enterprise keys have no expiry date — they are valid for the current major version of lpdf.

Do I need to rotate my license key?

Community and Professional require a new key each year — it deploys like any config value and renews automatically for paid plans. Enterprise keys are version-locked: the same key works until a new major version of lpdf is released, at which point you generate a new key from the portal as part of the upgrade. No calendar-driven rotation for Enterprise.

Is there a self-hosted option?

Lpdf runs entirely on your infrastructure — there's no cloud dependency and no data leaves your servers. Professional and Enterprise both include full self-hosted use.

How do I know which tier I need?

It depends on your organization's annual gross revenue: Community covers you under $1M, Professional from $1M to $10M, and Enterprise above $10M. All tiers include the full engine with no feature differences — the license is purely a compliance right. If you're unsure, start free and upgrade when you cross a threshold.
