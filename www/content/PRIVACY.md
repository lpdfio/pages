This Privacy Policy describes how Codesense LLC ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the lpdf.io website and the account portal at my.lpdf.io (collectively, the "Service").

## 2. What Data We Collect

### Account data

When you create an account at my.lpdf.io, we collect:

- Email address
- Name (optional, used for invoices)
- Password (stored as a salted hash — we never store your plaintext password)

### License and billing data

When you purchase a license, we collect and retain:

- License tier and purchase date
- License keys issued to your account
- Invoice history

Payment card details are processed directly by our payment processor (Stripe) and are never stored on our servers. We receive only a payment confirmation and a tokenized reference.

### Usage data

When you visit lpdf.io or my.lpdf.io, our hosting infrastructure may log:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and timestamps
- Referring URL

These logs are used for security monitoring and are retained for a maximum of 90 days.

### Support communications

If you contact us for support, we retain the content of that communication and your contact details for as long as needed to resolve your inquiry and for 12 months thereafter.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

- Create and manage your account
- Fulfill and manage license purchases
- Issue invoices and process renewals
- Provide customer support
- Send transactional emails (purchase confirmation, renewal reminders, license key delivery)
- Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell your data to third parties.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Account creation and license fulfillment | Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Renewal reminders and transactional emails | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| Security logging | Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| Compliance with legal obligations | Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) |

You may object to processing based on legitimate interests by contacting privacy@lpdf.io.

## 5. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following categories of third-party processors, under contracts that require them to protect your data:

- **Payment processor (Stripe):** processes payment card transactions
- **Transactional email provider:** delivers purchase confirmations and license keys
- **Hosting infrastructure:** serves the website and account portal

We do not share your data with any other third parties, and we do not sell your data.

If required by law or valid legal process (such as a court order), we may disclose data to law enforcement authorities. We will notify you of such a request where legally permitted to do so.

---

## 6. Data Retention

| Data type | Retention period |
|---|---|
| Account data | Until account deletion, plus 90 days |
| License and invoice records | 7 years (required for tax and accounting compliance) |
| Infrastructure access logs | 90 days |
| Support communications | 12 months after resolution |

When you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except for invoice and license records which are retained for the period required by law.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access:** request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectification:** request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure:** request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Portability:** request your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection:** object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Restriction:** request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@lpdf.io. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the EEA, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

## 8. Cookies

The lpdf.io marketing site uses no tracking cookies. We use only a single session cookie at my.lpdf.io to maintain your login state. This cookie is essential for the account portal to function and does not track you across other websites.

We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party tracking scripts.

## 9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

- HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
- Hashed and salted storage of passwords
- Access controls limiting who can access personal data
- Regular review of security practices

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

## 10. Children

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact privacy@lpdf.io and we will delete it.
